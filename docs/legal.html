2 · Editorial independence & accuracy

AI Press Review is an independent editorial project. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or representative of any AI company, research institution, or financial entity covered in its content.

Episodes are produced using an automated pipeline that collects and scores articles from 80+ publicly available RSS feeds, selects the most relevant items, and generates a script using a large language model. The final editorial decision — including release and publication — is made by the human editor, David Perron.

No warranty of accuracy. While every effort is made to present factual, verified information, AI Press Review makes no representation or warranty — express or implied — as to the completeness, accuracy, or timeliness of the content. Information may change rapidly in the AI sector. Listeners and readers are advised to verify information independently before acting on it.

AI Press Review does not provide financial, legal, medical, or investment advice. Nothing in any episode or on this website constitutes a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security or asset.
